Why Ravens?

I became a Baltimore Ravens fan in 2017 while working for Military AutoSource. Part of my job involved hosting marketing events, and a lot of those events included football games. Being around football more often got me interested in the NFL and I started following different teams.

When I decided to pick a team, I wanted one that was well run. I was looking for a franchise with a strong front office, good drafting, players with solid character, and a team that stayed competitive most seasons.

The Ravens checked all those boxes. They have a reputation for building strong teams through the draft and being one of the most stable organizations in the league. Once I started following them, I stuck with them and have been a fan ever since.

When I first started watching the Ravens, I remember really getting into players like Mark Andrews and Ronnie Stanley. Seeing young players develop and become important parts of the team made following the Ravens even more fun.

Later when I got into collecting sports cards, it made sense to build my collection around the team that got me into football in the first place. Most of the cards in my collection feature Ravens players from different eras of the franchise.
