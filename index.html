How I Got Into Sports Cards

I originally got into sports cards after watching videos on YouTube. Some of the channels that pulled me into the hobby were CardCollector2, King of the Kards, Sports Card Investor, and Chasing Cardboard. Watching those channels made the hobby look exciting and got me interested in opening packs and building a collection.


At the time I was already a big NFL fan, so football cards immediately caught my attention. One day I was at Walgreens and saw a pack of NFL cards sitting on the shelf. I decided to buy one just to see what it was like.


That first pack pretty much hooked me. Opening packs, seeing what players you pull, and building a collection is a lot of fun.

My collection now includes several binders full of cards, most of them focused on Baltimore Ravens players.
